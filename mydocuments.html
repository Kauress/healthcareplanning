Who is the best person to represent you, if there were a time when you couldn't make decisions or voice your medical preferences?

Best Person

What does "best person" mean? It means that you should select the person or people who will support the choices you make throughout this document.

  • For some people, that person os a family member. Others may think family members are too emotionally involved, so they would rather choose a friend or other loved one.
  • Your healthcare decision maker cannot be:
    • Under the age of 18
    • An employee or owner of a health or residential facility where you live, or a member of your medical team, unless they are related to you.
