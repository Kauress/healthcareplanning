WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW ABOUT LIFE SUPPORT

Life-prolongiing measures (also known as life support) help or replace a failing body function. These types of treatments can include breathing tubes (ventilator) and feeding tubes. Life-prolonging treatments can help keeps a person's body going while they heal from an illness and can help the body get stronger. They also keep a person alive for a long time even when there is no chance that they will recover.
